Privacy Policy — Battle Math

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Summary

Battle Math does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your child's privacy is completely protected.

Data Collection

Battle Math collects zero personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login required
  • No names, emails, or personal details collected
  • No analytics or tracking scripts
  • No cookies (except those required by the browser for PWA functionality)
  • No third-party services, ads, or SDKs
  • No network requests — the game works entirely offline

Local Storage

Game progress (scores, level completion, settings) is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Battle Math is designed for children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by simply not collecting any data at all. There is nothing to protect because nothing is collected.

Open Source

Battle Math is open source under the MIT License. You can inspect the complete source code at github.com/JesseRWeigel/battlemath to verify these claims.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub.
