Built for Learning

Battle Math uses research-backed educational techniques inspired by Alpha School's mastery-based learning approach.

🧠

Growth Mindset Feedback

Instead of "Wrong!", kids get encouraging hints: "Almost! Try again!" Progressive scaffolding helps them discover the answer.

📈

Adaptive Difficulty

Problems automatically adjust to your child's level. Too easy? It gets harder. Struggling? It scales back. Always in the learning zone.

⭐

Mastery-Based Progression

12 levels across 4 themed worlds. Kids must achieve 90%+ accuracy to advance — ensuring real understanding, not just guessing.

🏆

Motivating Rewards

Confetti celebrations, streak bonuses, star ratings, and boss battles keep kids engaged and wanting to practice more.

🌍

Available in 3 Languages

Play in English, Spanish, or French. Math is universal — the game should be too.

♿

Accessible to All

Screen reader support, keyboard navigation, high contrast mode, and large touch targets for all abilities.

What Kids Practice

➕

Addition

Sunny Meadow world. Easy (1-9), Medium (10-99), Hard (100-999).

➖

Subtraction

✖️

Multiplication

Magic Forest world. Times tables through three-digit numbers.

➗

Division

Crystal Cave world. Clean division with decimals mode available.
